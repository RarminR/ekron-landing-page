◆ The switch

Changing systems without holding your breath.

The fear is real: a switch that breaks the business. Here's how it actually goes.

STEP 01

We migrate your full history.

Every order, customer, SKU, price list and open balance — moved over, not left behind. Seventeen years of it, for the distributor already running on Ekron.

STEP 02

You verify your own numbers inside Ekron.

Before anything depends on it, you check your real data in the live system — open balances, last month's commissions, a price list you know by heart. You confirm it's right. We don't ask you to trust it; we ask you to check it.

STEP 03

You pick the day the old system goes dark.

Cutover happens on your date, not ours. Both systems run in parallel until you've seen Ekron hold for a full cycle — month-end included. No forced switch, no surprise weekend.

Until the day you choose, your old system stays on. You're never without a system that works.
